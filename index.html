personAbout Me

Hello! I'm Atul Jakhar, an Assistant Professor at Dayananda Sagar University, where I specialize in Computational Fluid Mechanics. My research explores the dynamic behavior of fluids through numerical methods and simulations, and I am currently delving into the exciting intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Fluid Mechanics to simplify and enhance problem-solving in this complex field.
Beyond academia, I'm a dedicated athlete who believes in the power of discipline and perseverance, both on the field and in life. I’m also passionate about inspiring others as a motivational speaker, sharing insights on personal growth, resilience, and the human potential to achieve excellence.
Welcome to my space, where science, sports, and self-mastery converge.
