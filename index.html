Atul Jakhar, Ph.D.
Assistant Professor
Dayananda Sagar University, Bengaluru, India
I am an Assistant Professor at Dayananda Sagar University, specializing in linear/nonlinear instability, heat and mass transfer, dynamical systems, and computational fluid dynamics. I hold an Integrated M.Sc. in Mathematics and a Ph.D. from the Central University of Rajasthan, where my doctoral research focused on the stability analysis of Newtonian fluids.

Beyond academia, I am passionate about teaching, mentoring young minds, and fostering curiosity-driven research. I also enjoy maintaining an active lifestyle as a dedicated athlete.
Research Interests:
• Linear/Nonlinear Instability
• Heat and Mass Transfer
• Dynamical Systems
• Computational Fluid Dynamics
• Bio-convection Phenomena
• Stability Analysis of Newtonian Fluids
