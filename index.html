👋 About me

    I am an Assistant Professor at Dayananda Sagar University, specializing in Linear/nonlinear instability, heat and mass transfer, dynamical systems, and computational fluid dynamics. I holds an Integrated M.Sc. in Mathematics; and a Ph.D. from the Central University of Rajasthan. My Ph.D. research explores the stability analysis of Newtonian fluid. With a strong research background, I worked extensively on nonlinear instability analysis and bio-convection phenomena, particularly in Newtonian fluids
